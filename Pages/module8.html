  2. Select a project team and submit an initial proposal here.
  3. Initial 'client' check-in meeting: Nov 6
  4. Submit "Problem Analysis" and "Planning" phases: Nov 16. (Module 4 Assignment I)
  5. Present software product to client (our class)
  6. Submit a formal written report. Here's a template to guide you.
